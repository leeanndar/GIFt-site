A three week itinerary for the perfect vacation!

(Hover over the photos to animate them!)

Canggu

A walkable surf city with great food and better waves. Enjoy the markets, beach clubs, and lively streets.

Ubud

Immerse yourself in life in the jungle - discover the waterfalls, rice fields, and monkey forest, and try out cooking and silversmith classes.

Lovina

Take a day trip to swim with the dolphins in deep ocean water.

Nusa Lembongan

This small island has beautiful blue beaches. Motorbike over the yellow bridge to nusa cheningan or take a quick boat to explore nusa penida.

Gili Trawangan

Rent a bike to circle the whole island and take a snorkeling trip to the neighboring Gili Meno and Gili Air. Head over to the west side to watch the sunset and then the east side for nightlife.

Uluwatu

Motorbike around this expansive city with cliffside shores, boutiques, and bars.
